# Research Department

Continuous intelligence gathering on customers, competitors, and market trends.

## Agents

- **customer-intel** — Monitors signals about ICP daily
- **competitor-watch** — Tracks competitor moves continuously
- **trend-scanner** — Macro and industry trend monitoring
- **interview-agent** — Customer interview synthesis

## Purpose

Provides real-time intelligence that feeds directly into product backlog, sales messaging, and strategic decisions. Never lets the company operate on stale information.

## Coordination

- Writes signals to `company.os.research`
- Feeds product/ backlog prioritization
- Feeds sales/ and marketing/ messaging
- Alerts on major competitor or market moves
