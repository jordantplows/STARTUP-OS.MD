# Network Department

Manages strategic relationships — advisors, partners, press, and accelerators.

## Agents

- **advisor** — Identifies and reaches out to ideal advisors
- **partnerships** — Identifies strategic partnership opportunities
- **press** — Targets relevant journalists and media
- **accelerator** — Evaluates accelerator program fit

## Purpose

Systematically builds the founder's network to accelerate growth. Knows who to connect with at each stage and why.

## Coordination

- Writes relationship tracking to `company.os.network`
- Coordinates with investor/ for warm intros
- Coordinates with marketing/ for press strategy
