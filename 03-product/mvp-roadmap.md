## Feature Prioritization Framework

### RICE Scoring Model
**Reach:** How many users will this impact in a given timeframe?
**Impact:** How much will this impact each user? (Massive = 3x, High = 2x, Medium = 1x, Low = 0.5x)
**Confidence:** How confident are we in our estimates? (High = 100%, Medium = 80%, Low = 50%)
**Effort:** How many person-weeks will this take?

**RICE Score = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort**
