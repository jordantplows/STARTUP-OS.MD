2. Use pain points to validate problem-solution fit
3. Map features to specific gains for each persona

### For Marketing & Sales
1. Craft messaging that speaks to each persona's pain points
2. Highlight gains that matter most to each segment
3. Choose channels based on behavioral traits
4. Develop case studies that resonate with specific personas

### For Customer Success

2. Anticipate questions based on pain points
3. Proactively address concerns based on risk tolerance
4. Measure satisfaction against desired outcomes
