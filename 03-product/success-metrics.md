## Prioritization Framework

### RICE Scoring Model
We use the RICE framework to prioritize features and initiatives:

**Formula:** RICE Score = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort

#### Reach
How many users will this impact over a defined time period?
- **Scale:** Estimated number of users/transactions per quarter
- **Example:** 1000 users = 1000 points

#### Impact
How much will this impact each user?
- **Massive (3.0):** Transforms the core value proposition
- **High (2.0):** Significant improvement to key workflow
- **Medium (1.0):** Moderate improvement
- **Low (0.5):** Small improvement
- **Minimal (0.25):** Minor polish

#### Confidence
How confident are we in our estimates?
- **High (100%):** Strong data, validated assumptions
- **Medium (80%):** Some data, reasonable assumptions
- **Low (50%):** Limited data, unvalidated assumptions

#### Effort
How much time will this require from the team?
- **Scale:** Person-months
- **Example:** 2 person-months = 2.0
