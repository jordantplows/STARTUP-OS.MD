## Feature Prioritization

### Prioritization Framework: RICE Scoring

**Formula:** RICE Score = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort

#### Scoring Guide:
- **Reach:** Estimated number of users affected per time period
- **Impact:** Scale of 0.25 (Minimal) to 3 (Massive)
- **Confidence:** Percentage (100% = High, 80% = Medium, 50% = Low)
- **Effort:** Person-months of work
