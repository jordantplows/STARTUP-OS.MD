## Risks & Mitigations

### Risk: Race to the Bottom
- **Mitigation**: Focus on value differentiation, avoid competing purely on price
- **Fallback**: Introduce additional value-add features to justify pricing

### Risk: Competitor Price War
- **Mitigation**: Build defensible moats (network effects, switching costs)
- **Fallback**: Introduce performance-based pricing tied to customer outcomes

### Risk: Price Too High for Market
- **Mitigation**: Offer extended trials, ROI calculators, case studies
- **Fallback**: Introduce lower-priced tier with reduced features

### Risk: Price Too Low (Leaving Money on Table)
- **Mitigation**: Regular price testing, willingness-to-pay surveys
- **Fallback**: Introduce premium features and higher tiers
