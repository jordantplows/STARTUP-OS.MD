## Fundraising Deck Outline

### Slide-by-Slide Structure (10-15 slides)

#### Slide 1: Cover
- Company name & logo
- Tagline: [One-sentence value proposition]
- Contact: [Founder name, email, date]
- **Key Message:** Make a strong first impression

#### Slide 2: Problem
- **Headline:** [Clear problem statement]
- **Content:**
  - 3-4 bullet points describing the pain
  - Current solutions and why they fail
  - Market evidence (stats, quotes)
- **Visual:** Customer pain visualization or stat callout
- **Key Message:** This is a painful, urgent problem worth solving

#### Slide 3: Solution
- **Headline:** [How you solve the problem]
- **Content:**
  - Product description in plain language
  - 3-5 key features/benefits
  - Why now? (timing, technology enablers)
- **Visual:** Product screenshot or demo
- **Key Message:** Our solution is 10x better than alternatives

#### Slide 4: Product Demo
- **Headline:** [How it works]
- **Content:**
  - Visual walkthrough (3-4 screenshots)
  - Customer workflow/use case
  - Key differentiators highlighted
- **Key Message:** The product is real and customers love it

#### Slide 5: Market Size
- **Headline:** [$X Billion Market Opportunity]
- **Content:**
  - TAM (Total Addressable Market): $[X]B
  - SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market): $[X]B

- **Headline:** [Pricing & Monetization]
- **Content:**
  - Pricing tiers summary
  - Revenue streams
  - Unit economics snapshot (LTV:CAC, payback)
- **Visual:** Pricing table or metrics
- **Key Message:** Clear path to profitability with strong economics

#### Slide 7: Traction
- **Headline:** [Growth trajectory or key milestone]
- **Content:**

  - Customer logos or testimonials
  - Product milestones
- **Visual:** Growth chart
- **Key Message:** We have momentum and product-market fit

#### Slide 8: Go-to-Market Strategy
- **Headline:** [How we acquire customers]
- **Content:**
  - Customer segments and ICPs
  - Acquisition channels (ranked by efficiency)
  - Sales process and cycle time
  - Partnerships and distribution
- **Key Message:** Repeatable, scalable customer acquisition

#### Slide 9: Competition
- **Headline:** [Competitive Landscape]

  - Competitive matrix (2x2 or feature comparison)

  - Why you win

- **Key Message:** We have a defensible competitive advantage

#### Slide 10: Team
- **Headline:** [World-class team]

### Deck Best Practices
- Keep to 10-12 slides for pitch, 15-20 with appendix
- Tell a story, not just facts
- Use visuals > text
- Focus on momentum and traction
- Tailor deck to audience (seed vs. growth, different investors)
- Update monthly with latest metrics
