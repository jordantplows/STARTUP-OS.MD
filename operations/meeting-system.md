## Instructions

Design a lightweight, effective meeting system for the startup:

1. **Standing Meetings**
   - Daily standups
   - Weekly team meetings
   - Monthly all-hands
   - Quarterly planning

2. **Meeting Principles**
   - Default to async when possible
   - Clear agenda required
   - Time-boxed
   - Action items documented

3. **Meeting Types**
   - Decision-making
   - Information sharing
   - Brainstorming
   - Retrospectives

4. **Tools & Templates**
   - Meeting note templates
   - Decision logs
   - Action item tracking

## Output Template

```markdown
# Meeting System — [Company Name]

## Philosophy

We default to asynchronous communication and hold meetings only when:
- Real-time discussion adds value
- Decision requires multiple perspectives
- Team alignment is needed
- Relationship building matters

## Standing Meetings

### Daily Standup (15 min)
- **When:** Every weekday, 9:30 AM
- **Who:** Engineering team
- **Format:** Round-robin updates
- **Questions:**
  - What did you ship yesterday?
  - What will you ship today?
  - Any blockers?

### Weekly Team Sync (30 min)
- **When:** Monday, 10 AM
- **Who:** Full team
- **Agenda:**
  - Wins from last week
  - Priorities this week
  - Help needed
  - Shoutouts

### Monthly All-Hands (60 min)
- **When:** First Monday of month, 4 PM
- **Who:** Everyone
- **Agenda:**
  - Company metrics
  - Product updates
  - Customer stories
  - Q&A

### Quarterly Planning (4 hours)
- **When:** Last week of quarter
- **Who:** Leadership + department leads
- **Agenda:**
  - Review quarterly OKRs
  - Set next quarter goals
  - Resource allocation
  - Strategic initiatives

## Meeting Guidelines

### Before the Meeting
1. Create agenda 24h in advance
2. Share relevant context
3. Assign facilitator & note-taker
4. Invite only essential attendees

### During the Meeting
1. Start on time
2. Follow the agenda
3. Capture action items
4. End with clear next steps

### After the Meeting
1. Share notes within 24h
2. Assign owners to action items
3. Set deadlines
4. Track in project management tool

## Meeting Types

### 1. Decision Meeting
- **Purpose:** Make a specific decision
- **Duration:** 30-45 min
- **Output:** Clear decision + rationale documented

### 2. Brainstorm
- **Purpose:** Generate ideas
- **Duration:** 45-60 min
- **Output:** List of ideas, no filtering yet

### 3. Retrospective
- **Purpose:** Reflect and improve
- **Duration:** 60 min
- **Framework:** Start/Stop/Continue

### 4. 1-on-1
- **Purpose:** Manager-report connection
- **Cadence:** Weekly or biweekly
- **Duration:** 30 min
- **Topics:** Career growth, feedback, concerns
```
