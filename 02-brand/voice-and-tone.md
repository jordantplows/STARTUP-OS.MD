## Content Review Checklist

Before publishing any content, verify:

### Voice Alignment
- [ ] Content reflects our [PLACEHOLDER: Core voice attributes]
- [ ] Tone is appropriate for the context and audience
- [ ] Language is consistent with brand personality

### Clarity
- [ ] Message is clear and easy to understand
- [ ] Jargon is explained or avoided
- [ ] Sentences are concise (average 15-20 words)
- [ ] Paragraphs are scannable (3-4 sentences max)

### Accuracy
- [ ] Facts and statistics are verified
- [ ] Links work and go to correct destinations
- [ ] Product/feature names are correct
- [ ] Legal/compliance requirements met

### Inclusivity
- [ ] Language is gender-neutral where appropriate
- [ ] Content is accessible to diverse audiences
- [ ] Cultural sensitivity maintained
- [ ] No assumptions about technical knowledge

### Consistency
- [ ] Terminology matches style guide
- [ ] Formatting follows brand standards
- [ ] Capitalization is correct
- [ ] Grammar and punctuation follow guidelines
