# User Personas

> Detailed profiles of your target customers that inform product decisions, marketing messaging, and sales strategy.

---

## Overview

User personas are research-based archetypes representing your core customer segments. They go beyond demographics to capture goals, pain points, behaviors, and buying patterns. Use these personas to:

- Prioritize product features based on real customer needs
- Craft messaging that resonates with each segment
- Design user experiences that match mental models
- Align sales and customer success around customer journeys

- `product-spec.md` - Feature requirements tied to persona needs
- `../01-foundation/value-proposition.md` - Value props tailored to each persona

## Using These Personas

### Product Development

**Feature prioritization:**
- Map each feature request to persona needs (Which persona(s) care most? Does it solve a top-3 pain point?)
- Use persona goals to define success metrics for features
- Test prototypes with real people who match persona profiles

**User experience design:**
- Design workflows that match persona mental models and tech stack
- Use language and terminology each persona uses (e.g., "forecast accuracy" for CRO, "data pipeline" for RevOps)
- Build role-based views (simple for end users, powerful for power users)

---

### Marketing & Messaging

**Content strategy:**
- Create content for each stage of each persona's buying journey
- Use persona-specific channels (e.g., Pavilion for sales leaders, RevOps Co-op for ops professionals)
- Feature real customers who match persona profiles in case studies

**Messaging hierarchy:**
- Lead with economic buyer (CRO) messaging in high-level positioning
- Create persona-specific landing pages and email campaigns
- Tailor demo scripts to persona priorities

---

### Sales Strategy

**Lead qualification:**
- Identify which persona is champion vs. economic buyer
- Ask qualifying questions based on persona pain points
- Look for trigger events that indicate buying window (e.g., missed forecast, new CRO hire)

**Demo customization:**
- Show features that matter most to the persona in the room
- Use terminology and examples relevant to their role
- Anticipate objections based on persona concerns

**Reference selection:**
- Match reference customers to prospect's persona and industry
- Prep references to speak to persona-specific pain points and outcomes
