## Product Strategy

### Problem We're Solving

**Primary problem:**

[PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Revenue leaders at B2B companies waste 10-15 hours per week manually building forecasts in spreadsheets, and those forecasts are still inaccurate 40% of the time. This leads to missed targets, poor resource planning, and loss of board/investor confidence."]

**Why this problem matters:**

[PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Forecast accuracy directly impacts a company's ability to plan hiring, spending, and growth. A 20% forecast miss can mean layoffs, missed board targets, and down rounds. Revenue leaders are measured on their ability to hit numbers - inaccurate forecasts put their jobs at risk."]

**Why existing solutions fall short:**

[PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Current alternatives include: (1) Manual spreadsheets - time-consuming, error-prone, not real-time; (2) CRM reporting - limited analytics, doesn't account for deal velocity or pipeline health; (3) Legacy revenue intelligence platforms - expensive ($100K+), require months to implement, built for enterprise not mid-market."]

---

### Target Market

**Primary ICP (Ideal Customer Profile):**

[PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Mid-market B2B SaaS companies with $10M-$50M ARR, 50-200 employees, selling to other businesses with ACV of $20K-$100K, using Salesforce as CRM, with a dedicated RevOps person or team."]

**Secondary markets:**

[PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Can expand to (1) smaller companies ($5M-$10M ARR) with simpler needs, or (2) larger companies ($50M-$100M ARR) with more complex requirements. Can also expand beyond SaaS to other B2B industries (manufacturing, professional services) with similar sales processes."]

**Anti-ICP (who we're NOT building for):**

[PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "B2C companies, transactional/e-commerce businesses, companies with sales cycles under 30 days, enterprise companies with $500M+ ARR (they need different level of customization and support)."]
