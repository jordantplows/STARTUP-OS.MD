## Template Notes (Delete before posting)

**Voice and tone**: This JD should sound like your brand. Reference `02-brand/voice-and-tone.md` for:
- Active voice ("You'll ship features" not "Features will be shipped")
- Direct language (cut buzzwords like "synergy," "rockstar," "ninja")
- Honest and human (not corporate HR-speak)

**Salary transparency**: If you're in a location requiring salary transparency (CA, CO, NY, WA, etc.), this is legally required. Even if not required, it's best practice—it saves time and builds trust.

**Values integration**: Pull 3-4 values from `culture-doc.md` and weave them into the "Who You Are" and "What Makes Us Different" sections. Candidates should self-select based on values fit.

**Length**: Aim for 800-1200 words. Long enough to be comprehensive, short enough to hold attention.

**SEO**: Include keywords candidates search for (e.g., job title variations, tech stack, remote/location).

**Updates**: Review and update quarterly to reflect current team size, product stage, and benefits.
