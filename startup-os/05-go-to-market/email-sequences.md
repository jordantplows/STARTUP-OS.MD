# Email Sequences

> Production-ready email drip campaigns with full copy for cold outbound, trial nurture, and customer onboarding.

---

## Overview

This document contains 5 complete email sequences:

1. **Cold Outbound Sequence** (Tier 2/3 prospects) - 5 emails over 14 days
2. **Dream 100 Sequence** (Tier 1 prospects) - 6 touchpoints over 20 days
3. **Trial Nurture Sequence** - 7 emails over 14-day trial
4. **Customer Onboarding Sequence** - 5 emails over first 30 days
5. **Press Outreach Sequence** - 3 emails for media/journalists

## Email Best Practices Summary

### Subject Lines

**High-performing formats**:

- [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Quick question, {{FirstName}}" (curiosity)]
- [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "How {{CompanyName}} cut churn by 30%" (social proof)]
- [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Should I close your file?" (breakup)]
- [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "{{FirstName}}, did you see this?" (personal)]

**Avoid**:

- [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Re: Following up" (too generic)]
- [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Introducing [Product]" (too salesy)]
- [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "You're going to love this" (too hype)]

---

### Email Body

**Do's**:

- ✅ [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Keep it under 100 words (people skim)"]
- ✅ [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Lead with their problem, not your product"]
- ✅ [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "One clear CTA (don't give multiple options)"]
- ✅ [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Use 'you' more than 'we'"]
- ✅ [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Add a P.S. (highest-read part of email)"]

**Don'ts**:

- ❌ [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Don't use marketing buzzwords ('synergy', 'cutting-edge', 'revolutionary')"]
- ❌ [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Don't write paragraphs (use bullets or short lines)"]
- ❌ [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Don't be vague ('Let me know if you're interested')"]
- ❌ [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Don't talk about yourself (focus on them)"]

---

### CTAs

**High-converting CTAs**:

- [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Worth a 15-min call to explore?" (soft ask)]
- [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Grab time here: [Calendly link]" (frictionless)]
- [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Want to see how we'd do this for {{Company}}?" (personalized)]
- [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Reply with 'yes' and I'll send it over" (low friction)]

**Avoid**:

- [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Let me know if you're interested" (too passive)]
- [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Can we schedule a demo?" (too formal)]
- [PLACEHOLDER: E.g., "Click here to learn more" (too vague)]
