## Voice Checklist

Before publishing any customer-facing content, check:

- [ ] Would I say this out loud to a customer? (If no, rewrite)
- [ ] Did I use the simplest word possible? (No "utilize" when "use" works)
- [ ] Did I cut everything that doesn't add value? (Kill your darlings)
- [ ] Is it clear what action the reader should take? (Don't make them guess)
- [ ] Does it sound like *us*? (Read it out loud — does it sound like your brand personality?)
