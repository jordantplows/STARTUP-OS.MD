## Messaging Do's and Don'ts

### DO

- ✅ Lead with the customer's problem, not your product
- ✅ Use concrete numbers and outcomes, not vague promises
- ✅ Speak in your customer's language, not internal jargon
- ✅ Focus on outcomes ("Reduce churn by 30%") not features ("ML-powered dashboard")
- ✅ Test messages with real customers before committing

### DON'T

- ❌ Use buzzwords like "innovative," "cutting-edge," "revolutionary"
- ❌ Say you're "the leading" or "the best" without proof
- ❌ Focus on how hard you worked to build the product
- ❌ Assume prospects understand your acronyms or insider terms
- ❌ Copy competitor messaging (even if it's working for them)
