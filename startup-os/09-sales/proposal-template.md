## Proposal Writing Principles

### Key Guidelines

1. **Customer-Centric:** Start with their problem, not your product
2. **Quantified Value:** Include specific, measurable ROI and outcomes
3. **Evidence-Based:** Use proof points (customer stories, data, references)
4. **Clear Pricing:** Transparent, detailed pricing breakdown (no surprises)
5. **Action-Oriented:** Clear next steps and mutual commitments
6. **Professional:** Well-formatted, error-free, branded consistently
7. **Concise:** 8-12 pages max (exec summary, business case, pricing, next steps)

**Required Inputs:**
